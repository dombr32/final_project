About me

I’m a Product Designer based in Melbourne, Australia. I enjoy working on product design, design systems, and Webflow projects, but I don’t take myself too seriously. I’ve worked with some of the world’s most exciting companies, including Coinbase, Stripe, and Linear. I’m passionate about helping startups grow, improve their UX and customer experience, and to raise venture capital through good design. My work has been featured on Typewolf, Mindsparkle Magazine, Webflow, Fonts In Use, CSS Winner, httpster, Siteinspire, and Best Website Gallery.
